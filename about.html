about me.

Currently, I'm a Computer Science major at Texas A&M University in College Station (Class of 2023). My interests are broad, but lately I've become particularly interested in front-end development and UI/UX design. I'm passionate about technology that people use daily and that makes their lives easier.

On teams, I feel a strong sense of responsibility not to let my teammates down. I'm self-motivated and I work well under pressure. I value a corporate culture that embraces work-life balance, respect, and open communication.

Outside of coding, I like to read, listen to music, binge-watch Youtube videos, and recently I started learning how to draw digitally.
